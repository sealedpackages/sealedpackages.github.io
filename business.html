FAQ

How does it work?

We’ll stage your product at a centrally-located facility. When an order is placed, your customer will get to select a delivery window. We’ll fullfill and complete the delivery on your behalf.

Why is your service unique?

Lots of reasons! Here are a few:
  • Consumers get to schedule delivery when it is convenient, providing a great experience while elminating costs associated with theft and missed delivery attempts.
  • Brands love the opportunties to control the delivery experience ing opportunities at the point of delivery. Want something special and unique to your brand to happen during delivery? We’ll make it happen.
  • Because we strategically locate product within a city, we’re able to deliver faster than anyone else.
  • Want to offer returns? Need refridgeration? Box recycling? We do it all.

How can you offer a service that is both better and cheaper?

Our service is unique in that we allow the consumer to schedule deliveries from the brands they love, at times that are convenient for them. This reduces the number of redeliveries and package thefts, making the service more affordable than traditional options.

Do you offer fullfillment?

Yes, we can do fullfillment as well as delivery.

How much does it cost?

We have several different pricing models, though we usually bill on a “per-item” basis. For specific pricing questions, please reach out to us at hello@sealedpackages.com.

What is your current coverage in the Bay Area?

Currrently, Sealed operates in San Francisco with plans to expand in the near future.

Who actually does the delivery, and how do they do it?

Our highly-trained couriers complete all deliveries using the vehicle best suited to the job. The majority of our dense, urban deliveries are completed via bicycle (often cheaper and faster!), but we use cars and trucks when required.

When can I schedule my deliveries?

We currently offer evening deliveries 7 days a week, from 5pm to 11pm. You may select the delivery window that fits best with your schedule.
