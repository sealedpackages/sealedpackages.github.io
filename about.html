Our story.

Sealed was founded by two aerospace engineers frustrated with inconvenient and unsafe package delivery in San Francisco.

Traditional mail carriers attempt midday deliveries when nobody is home, and they leave packages in unsafe locations for thieves to swipe. Rescheduling deliveries is annoying, and each carrier has their own redelivery policies, driver instructions, access points, missed delivery notices, mobile apps, phone support, and fine print. You just want your package!

Our principles.

Convenience: With Sealed, you pick a 1-hour window for delivery that works for you. You won’t need to stay at home all day for a UPS delivery that says “Today before 8pm” or miss an attempted Fedex delivery at 1pm while you’re at work. Tell us when to deliver your packages, and we’ll be there.

Flexibility: Headed out of town for the weekend and worried about online orders? You shouldn’t have to coordinate your plans around delivery estimates. Sealed receives and keeps your packages safe until you’re ready for them.

Security: Package theft is growing and many residents are finding their doorstep or gate is not a safe place for packages. You shouldn’t have to rush to your door, run home from work, or install expensive cameras just to thwart thieves.

Trust: Receiving your packages is not only important to you, but it’s also very personal. We’re continually humbled by the number of people that trust us to do this important job. If you haven’t already, we’re excited for you to join the Sealed family.

Your $12 order of food is delivered into your hands, but your $150 Amazon package is left unattended at your doorstep “sometime before 8pm”? Huh?
