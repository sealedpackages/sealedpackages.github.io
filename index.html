Frequently asked questions

What is a Harbor?

A Harbor is a secure access point near your home where you can choose to have each new package routed to. You're notifed as soon as it's available for pickup. We’re launching new Harbors all the time, so stay updated by signing up below.

Where are these Harbors located?

Our Harbors are local businesses in your area that partner with Sealed and are dedicated to the security of your packages. We won't offer Harbors services in your neighborhood until we know you'll have a secure location within a 5-minute walk. Signup below to see which one is closest to you!

Should I route my package to a Harbor or order a home handoff?

You choose! We can bring your packages wherever you want. Picking up your packages at a nearby Harbor is the most affordable, while home handoffs are the most convenient. Both are safe and secure, eliminating the chance of theft.

How much does it cost?

Check out our Pricing Section below.

Our main Sealed service is only $9 per month, which includes the first 4 packages - all routed to your favorite Harbor. Additional packages handled each month are only $2 each. There is an additional fee to schedule a package handoff at your home, which range from $5-9 depending on package size and time of day. Oversized item fees will also apply to packages greater than 50 lbs or 60 inches in size.

We hate surprise fees as much as you! We'll never charge you before first notifying you of the costs via text or mobile app and getting your confirmation for the additional services. Picking up your package directly from our service centers will always be a free alternative to Harbor routing and home handoffs.

Is this like a PO box or an Amazon locker?

Sealed is better than a PO box. PO boxes are more expensive, limited in size, can’t accept most packages, and don’t provide you with smart notifications. Getting the contents of a PO box delivered to home is very expensive and not offered as an on-demand service.

I’m already offered free shipping from a retailer or shipping company. Why should I pay for this?

Unfortunately, free shipping still doesn’t mean it’s safely in your hands at the end of the day. Package theft is on the rise, and these “porch pirates” are getting more opportunities as online shopping grows. These pirates on the high seas (of your neighborhood) rarely get caught in the nets of justice (less than 10% are caught). If you’re tired of being left on-the-hook for stolen packages, and dealing with delays from missed deliveries, sign up for Sealed today!

Do I have to use a specific shipping company?

No. What makes us different from the lockers or access points provided by shipping companies is that you don't need to navigate separate websites, lookup availability, and update shipping information each time. We accept your packages from anybody and anywhere. Just ship directly to us, and we’ll get it to you.

My package is time-sensitive. Will using Sealed slow down delivery?

Not at all! Your package is available for pickup or handoff the same day that we receive it. Sealed protects your package against thefts, and delays from missed deliveries, so you’ll always receive your items securely and reliably. Our seals make sure that you're never left out to dry.

Where is Sealed currently operating?

We’re rapidly expanding our service, primarily in the city of San Francisco. To check if your neighborhood is covered, signup below. As we get closer to you, we'll offer access to our treasure map of Harbors so you can decide if Sealed makes sense for you.
