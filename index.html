Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost?

Check out our Pricing Section below.

Is this like a PO box or an Amazon locker?

Sealed is better than a PO box. PO boxes are expensive and don’t provide you with smart notifications or convenient delivery options. Amazon lockers only hold your Amazon orders. They’re often full, can’t accept large packages, and can have limited service hours.

I’m already offered free shipping from a retailer or shipping company. Why should I pay for this?

Unfortunately, free shipping still doesn’t mean it’s safely in your hands at the end of the day. Most failed delivery attempts occur in the middle of the day when you are at work, and package theft is on the rise as online shopping grows. If you’re tired of dealing with missed deliveries or stolen packages, Sealed is a convenient option to get your items securely delivered to you on your schedule.

Do I have to use a specific shipping company?

No. What makes us different from the lockers or access points provided by shipping companies is that you don't need to navigate separate websites, lookup availability, and update shipping information each time. We accept your packages from anybody and anywhere. Just ship directly to us, and we’ll get it to you.

My package is time-sensitive. Will using Sealed slow down delivery?

No. Your package is available for pickup or handoff the same day that we receive it. Sealed protects your package against thefts, and delays from missed deliveries, so you’ll always receive your items securely and reliably.

Can I use Sealed to receive packages from a meal-kit service (i.e. Blue Apron, HelloFresh, etc...)?

Yes, we can receive these packages and deliver them same-day to your home.

Where is Sealed currently operating?

We’re rapidly expanding our service in the city of San Francisco. To check if your neighborhood is covered, sign up below.

When can I schedule my deliveries?

We currently offer evening deliveries 7 days a week, from 5pm to 11pm. You may select the delivery window that fits best with your schedule.

I have ordered multiple packages. Can I have them brought to me all at once to save on delivery costs?

Yes, and you can save money by grouping multiple packages into one scheduled delivery trip. Please check out the Pricing Section below for more information.

What is a scheduled delivery trip?

A delivery trip is when you schedule a time for Sealed to come to your door and hand your packages to you. The cost of each delivery trip is independent of the number of packages that we are bringing. For instance, if we bring three of your packages at once, that delivery trip will only cost you $3.50 if you're a Sealed Member or $5 if you're on Anyone Pricing.

How do I get started?

Start by signing up below. Once you have made an account and picked a plan, you’re ready to start having orders shipped to the address shown in the app. We’ll take care of the rest by notifying you when we receive a package and providing you with convenient delivery scheduling options.
