COVID-19 Updates

With the ongoing COVID-19 outbreak and San Francisco’s new Shelter-in-Place order , we want you to know that Sealed is still operating as an essential service. This means that we are continuing to receive packages and offer evening deliveries 7 days a week. We are also following the CDC Guidance for Businesses to protect the health of the whole Sealed family.

Beyond our existing package service, we are looking at offering more services to help connect you with local businesses who need your support. One way we can do this is by providing affordable same-day delivery for local products. Let us know if there’s a local business that you would love to have delivery from and we’ll try our best to make it happen!

For your own safety, we encourage you to follow the CDC and the City of San Francisco guidelines for important updates and information.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don’t hesitate to reach out!

- The Sealed Team
